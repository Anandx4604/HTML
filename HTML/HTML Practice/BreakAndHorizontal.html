Weeping

by Federico GarcAa Lorca

Weeping,
I go down the street
Grotesque, without the solution
With the sadness of Cyrano
And Quixote.

String Bass

by Adrian Green

Some like to dominate.
others caress
a voluptuous rhythm
on pliant strings.

This pulse drives life
through wanton counterpoint
the heart and harmony
of things.
